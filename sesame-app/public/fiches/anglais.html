Anglais
40 questions · 30 min Temps, conditionnels, culture UK Faux-amis à maîtriser
Notions clés
🕐
Les temps essentiels
  • Present simple → habitude / vérité générale (I work)
  • Present continuous → action en cours / futur proche (I am working)
  • Present perfect → lien présent-passé : just / already / never (I have just finished)
  • Past simple → moment précis révolu (I worked yesterday)
  • Past perfect → avant une action passée (I had worked before she arrived)
🔀
Les 3 conditionnels
  • Type 1 (réel) : if + present → will — "If it rains, I will stay"
  • Type 2 (potentiel) : if + were/past → would — "If I were rich, I would travel"
  • Type 3 (irréel passé) : if + had+PP → would have — "If I had known, I would have come"
  • Type 2 → toujours were (jamais was) pour tous les pronoms
⚠️
Faux-amis classiques
  • surname = nom de famille (≠ surnom → nickname)
  • eventually = finalement (≠ éventuellement → possibly)
  • actually = en fait (≠ actuellement → currently)
  • library = bibliothèque (≠ librairie → bookshop)
  • sensible = raisonnable (≠ sensible → sensitive)
🔧
Adjectifs -ed / -ing
  • -ed = ressenti par quelqu'un : I am bored / tired / interested
  • -ing = caractéristique de quelque chose : The film is boring / tiring
  • Piège : "I am boring" = je suis ennuyeux (pas ennuyé !)
  • Astuce : -ed = moi, -ing = la chose
🔗
Prépositions clés
  • Adj+prep : proud OF, afraid OF, good AT, interested IN, responsible FOR
  • Nom+prep : lack OF, reason FOR, advantage OF
  • Verbe+prep : succeed IN, insist ON, depend ON, look forward TO+ing
🇬🇧
UK & ses nations
  • Angleterre + Écosse + Pays de Galles + Irlande du Nord = Royaume-Uni
  • Dévolution = transfert de pouvoirs aux parlements régionaux
  • Holyrood = Parlement écossais / Stormont = Irlande du Nord
  • Brexit = sortie de l'UE effective en 2020
  • BBC = service public audiovisuel britannique
📝
Phrasal verbs fréquents
  • look up = chercher (dictionnaire)
  • look for = chercher (objet, personne)
  • look forward to = anticiper avec plaisir (+ -ing)
  • give up = abandonner / put off = reporter
  • carry out = effectuer / bring up = évoquer, élever

Conjugaison structurée
📅 Tableau des temps
Present simple → I work / He works Present continuous → I am working Present perfect → I have worked Past simple → I worked Past perfect → I had worked Future will → I will work Future going to → I am going to work
Repère : present perfect = toujours avec have/has + participe passé. Pas de date précise → ≠ past simple.
❓ Former des questions
Ouverte → What do/did/have you...? Fermée → Do/Did/Have you...? Tag → You work here, don't you? → She didn't come, did she? Indirecte→ Can you tell me where he lives? → (pas d'inversion sujet-verbe)
Piège questions indirectes : après Can you tell me... → ordre affirmatif (where he lives, pas does he live).
🔄 Voix passive
Actif → Orwell wrote the book. Passif → The book was written by Orwell. Structure : be + past participle Present → is/are + PP Past → was/were + PP Future → will be + PP Agent (BY) = optionnel si évident
Astuce : le complément d'objet direct devient sujet. L'agent se place avec by en fin de phrase.
💬 Style indirect
"I work" → said he worked "I will go" → said he would go "I have done" → said he had done "I am going" → said he was going "I went" → said he had gone Adverbes : now → then today → that day tomorrow → the next day yesterday→ the day before
Règle : recul d'un temps dans le passé (backshift). Les adverbes de temps changent aussi.

Conditionnel type 2 → were pour tous les pronoms
❌ Incorrect
"The news is bored"
✅ Correct
"The news is boring"
-ing = ce qui provoque l'ennui ; -ed = celui qui ressent l'ennui
❌ Incorrect
"He work every day" / "She go to school"
✅ Correct
"He works" / "She goes"
Présent simple → -s/-es obligatoire à la 3e personne du singulier
❌ Incorrect
"I have lived here since 3 years"
✅ Correct
"I have lived here for 3 years"
since = point de départ ("since 2020") · for = durée ("for 3 years")
❌ Incorrect
"She is actuellement in London" → "actually"
✅ Correct
"She is currently in London"
actually = en fait / currently = actuellement (faux-ami classique)
Quiz éclair — cliquer pour révéler
1. Translate: "Si j'avais su, je ne serais pas venu."
"If I had known, I would not have come."
→ Conditionnel type 3 : if + had + PP → would have + PP (irréel passé)
2. Give a synonym of "huge".
enormous, massive, vast, immense
→ Tous synonymes d'une très grande taille ou ampleur
3. What does "devolution" mean in the UK context?
Transfer of powers from Westminster to regional parliaments: Scottish Parliament (Holyrood), Welsh Senedd, and Northern Ireland Assembly (Stormont).
→ Ce n'est pas une décentralisation totale : le Parlement de Westminster reste souverain
4. Fill in the blank: "She finds the lecture ___ " (bore)
"She finds the lecture boring."
→ -ing car c'est la conférence qui provoque l'ennui (caractéristique de la chose)
5. Fill in: "I'm looking forward ___ seeing you."
"I'm looking forward to seeing you."
→ look forward TO + verbe en -ing (to fait partie du phrasal verb, pas un infinitif)
